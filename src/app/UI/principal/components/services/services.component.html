Responsible Adoption

At Milocaninos, we are dedicated to finding loving homes for dogs in need. We offer a comprehensive adoption service that includes home assessments, perfect matching between the dog and the family, and post-adoption follow-ups to ensure all the dog's needs are properly met.

We understand that older dogs require special care and a lot of love. We provide dedicated care services for elderly dogs, including regular veterinary attention, diets tailored to their needs, and a peaceful home environment for them to spend their golden years comfortably and affectionately.

Personalized Canine Training

We recognize the importance of having a well-behaved and socialized dog. We offer personalized training sessions for dogs of all ages and skill levels. Our expert trainers work with you and your dog to address behavior issues, teach basic commands, and encourage positive socialization.

Temporary and Emergency Care

We provide temporary and emergency care services for dogs whose owners are facing unexpected situations such as hospitalization, natural disasters, or displacement.
